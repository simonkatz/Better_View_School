Tell Me More

Day Care

From 8months to 2years old, we provide day care for parents who want their children to get a headstart. The earlier they start, the earlier they learn language and social skills while they play with the numerous toys we have.

Nursery

Our teachers approach educating our todlers with patience, love and care; just like a parent would do. We balance out class time with play time to give our pupils a chance to build social and communication skills as they grow.

Boarding

Our facilities can accomodate over 150 pupils with room for expansion already on plan. We encourage parents interested in the borading section to visit us for a personal tour and impression of our facilities.

Primary

Creating a well rounded student is what we strive for. We educate our children in and out of class and provide extra curricular activities such as debate, sports, swimming and cultural dances to further learning.

Aims:

Religion:

We consider religion to be a priority. We believe our God is a God of vision.

We have respect for all recorgnized religions and pupils are taught to be God fearing and God Loving.

Health:

The school has a qualified and experienced nurse to cater to the health of our pupils.

Each child is reqiured to come with an immunisation card to track health and development.

Security:

We have full time security guards to ensure the safety of our children and school property.

Transport:

The school currently has eight vans for transporting our children to and from school.
