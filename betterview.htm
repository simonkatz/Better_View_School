Primary School

Creating a well rounded pupil is what we strive for. We educate our children in and out of class.
We have extra curricular activities such as debate, sports, swimming and cultural dances to further learning.

Nursery School

Our teachers approach educating our toddlers with patience, love and care; just like a parent would.
We balance out class time with play time to build social and communication skills as they grow.

Day Care

From 8months to 2years old, we provide day care for parents who want their children to get a headstart. The earlier they start, the earlier they learn language and social skills while they play with the numerous toys we have.

Boarding

Our facilities can accomodate over 150 pupils with room for expansion already on plan. We encourage parents interested in the borading section to visit us for a personal tour and impression of our school.

We are Better View School.

Our goal is to provide a culture of academic excellence and instill within pupils a love of learning academically and socially.
We strive to empower them to be responsible, contributing members of our community.

AIMS

Meeting the elementary education needs of the next generation in our economy. Creating a safe and fun learning environment while providing jobs for qualified Ugandans.

RELIGION

We consider religion to be a priority. We believe our God is a God of vision. We have respect for all recorgnized religions and pupils are taught to be God fearing and God Loving

HEALTH

The school has a qualified and experienced nurse to cater to the health of our pupils. Each child is reqiured to come with an immunisation card to track health and development.
